Kevin's CS70 Notes

This is a very rough version of a website I intend on making, but for now bear with me please :). Below are links to a compilation of notes that I think could be useful in your adventure through CS70. I wrote the majority of these notes by hand during the course, and adapted them so that they look cleaner to the eye. These notes are essentially the SparkNotes of your course notes, as well as a few tips and tricks that I found useful through my readings of CS70 TA + CSM materials. These notes are not perfect, and assume that you have already read through the course notes, and are just using these to refresh your memory and solidify a few concepts. This is no substitute to practice, but more of a checklist of what you should know. The notes are not perfect, and I will update them periodically. Please do not hesitate to reach out to me at kevinliu743@berkeley.edu or complete this form if you have any questions and/or suggestions.
